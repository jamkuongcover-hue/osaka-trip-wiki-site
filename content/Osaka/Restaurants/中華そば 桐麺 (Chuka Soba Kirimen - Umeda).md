# 中華そば 桐麺 (Chuka Soba Kirimen - Umeda)

- **Name**: 中華そば 桐麺 (Chuka Soba Kirimen - Umeda)
- **Address**: 大阪府大阪市北区大深町1-1 ヨドバシ梅田塔 B1F LINKS UMEDA (LINKS UMEDA B1F)
- **Business Hours**: 11:00–23:00 (Check official Instagram @kirimen_official for updates)
- **Price**: ~1000-1500 yen
- **Signature Dish**: 'Kiritama' (桐玉) - A simple yet stunning egg-mixed noodle dish. Highly elastic and chewy noodles served with a high-quality raw egg and special sauce.
- **Other Dishes**: Kotteri Tsukemen (Rich dipping noodles).
- **Charlotte's Tip**: The 'Kiritama' is famous for its 'noodle-only' confidence. You eat the noodles with just the egg and sauce to appreciate the incredible texture. The new branch in LINKS UMEDA is super convenient right next to Osaka/Umeda station!

# Sukiyaki Kitamura

A Michelin 1-star sukiyaki specialty restaurant founded in 1881.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), elderly-friendly.
- **Accessibility**: Traditional setting, but private rooms are available. Recommended to check elevator access for specific floors.
- **Parking**: Use nearby paid parking lots (Shinsaibashi area).
- **Food Type**: Kansai-style Sukiyaki (High-quality Wagyu cooked in a pot).
- **Note**: Requires advance reservation. Very famous for its "melt-in-your-mouth" beef.

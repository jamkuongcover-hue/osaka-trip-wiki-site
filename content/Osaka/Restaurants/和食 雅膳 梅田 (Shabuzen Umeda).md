# Shabuzen Umeda

A restaurant specializing in premium Wagyu shabu-shabu and sukiyaki in the heart of Umeda.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), family-friendly (private rooms), elderly-friendly.
- **Accessibility**: Located in the Hilton Plaza West (generally excellent accessibility standards).
- **Parking**: On-site parking available within Hilton Plaza West.
- **Food Type**: Shabu-shabu and Sukiyaki (High-quality Wagyu cooked in a pot).
- **Note**: Offers private dining rooms, making it a comfortable experience for families and the elderly.

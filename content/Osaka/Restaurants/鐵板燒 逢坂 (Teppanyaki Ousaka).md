# Teppanyaki OUSAKA (Hotel Plaza Osaka)

A teppanyaki restaurant on the top floor of Hotel Plaza Osaka offering Omi beef and city views.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka (Juso)
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), Omakase-style experience, family-friendly.
- **Accessibility**: Located on the 19th floor of Hotel Plaza Osaka. Full elevator access and hotel-standard accessible facilities.
- **Parking**: On-site hotel parking available.
- **Food Type**: Teppanyaki (Omi Beef, one of the top three Wagyu brands).
- **Note**: Panoramic night views of Osaka. Multilingual staff available.

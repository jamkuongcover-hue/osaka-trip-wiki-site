# 麺に光を 別邸 (Ramen Menhika Branch)

- **地址**: 日本〒556-0011 Osaka, Naniwa Ward, Nanbanaka, 1 Chome−18−8 難波カルチャービル 1F (Near Namba Station)
- **營業時間**: 11:30–15:00, 18:00–21:00 (Check X/Twitter @menhika02 for irregular holidays)
- **特色**: Specialized in 'Shellfish Broth' (Kai-no-Hikari) and 'Seafood Soy Sauce' (Dashi-no-Hikari). Known for deep, clear, non-greasy broth with a strong oyster/shellfish flavor. Elegant presentation.
- **價格**: ~1050 yen per bowl.

## Charlotte's Tip
Perfect if you're tired of heavy, fatty pork bone (tonkotsu) broths. The oyster flavor is incredibly fresh and clear. It's a hidden gem near Namba that doesn't usually have crazy long queues like the famous ones.

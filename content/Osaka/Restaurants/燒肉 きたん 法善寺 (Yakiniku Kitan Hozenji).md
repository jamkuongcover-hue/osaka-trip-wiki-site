# Yakiniku Kitan (Hozenji)

A calm Wagyu restaurant located in a renovated 120-year-old building in Hozenji Yokocho.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka (Namba)
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), Omakase-style experience, elderly-friendly atmosphere.
- **Accessibility**: Renovated traditional building. Offers a calm, quiet atmosphere. Private rooms available.
- **Parking**: Nearby paid parking lots in Namba.
- **Food Type**: Yakiniku (Aged Wagyu, Matsusaka beef yaki-suki).
- **Note**: Famous for Chateaubriand and aged salted tongue.

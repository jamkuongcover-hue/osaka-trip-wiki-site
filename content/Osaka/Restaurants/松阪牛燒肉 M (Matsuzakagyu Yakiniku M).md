# Matsuzakagyu Yakiniku M (Hozenji Hanare)

Famous for serving top-tier Matsusaka Wagyu in various styles.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), family-friendly.
- **Accessibility**: Private rooms available, which is good for families and the elderly.
- **Parking**: Nearby paid parking lots in Namba/Dotonbori.
- **Food Type**: Yakiniku (Japanese JamQ) - you can cook it to your preference.
- **Note**: English and Chinese speaking staff available.

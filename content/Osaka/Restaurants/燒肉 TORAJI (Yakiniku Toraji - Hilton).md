# Yakiniku Toraji (Hilton Plaza Osaka West)

A famous Wagyu chain's high-end branch offering thick-cut beef in private rooms.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka (Umeda)
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), family-friendly (private rooms).
- **Accessibility**: Located in Hilton Plaza West. Excellent accessibility standards with elevators and accessible restrooms.
- **Parking**: On-site parking available in the building.
- **Food Type**: Yakiniku (Japanese JamQ). Known for thick-cut decadent slices.
- **Note**: Private rooms provide a comfortable space for families with children or the elderly.

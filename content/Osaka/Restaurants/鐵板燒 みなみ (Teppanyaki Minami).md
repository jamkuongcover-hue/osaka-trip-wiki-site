# Teppanyaki Minami (Swissotel Nankai Osaka)

A high-end teppanyaki restaurant that welcomes families and offers high-quality Wagyu.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Osaka
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), family-friendly (children allowed), elderly-friendly.
- **Accessibility**: Located on the 10th floor of Swissotel Nankai Osaka. Full hotel accessibility with elevators and accessible restrooms.
- **Parking**: On-site hotel parking available.
- **Food Type**: Teppanyaki (Cooked in front of you). High-quality Wagyu.
- **Note**: This is high-end dining, great for special occasions. Reservations are recommended.

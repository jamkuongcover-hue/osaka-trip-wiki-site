# NIFREL (Interactive Aquazoo)

An innovative "interactive aquazoo" that blends elements of an aquarium, zoo, and art museum.

## Details
- **Category**: Attractions
- **City**: Osaka (Suita)
- **Target Audience**: Family-friendly, elderly-friendly.
- **Accessibility**: Excellent. Fully accessible with elevators and flat floors. Stroller and wheelchair friendly.
- **Parking**: Large parking available at the EXPOCITY complex.
- **Highlights**: Unique exhibits categorized by "senses" (Colors, Abilities, Shapes, etc.), up-close animal encounters (lemurs, birds roaming freely), and the "Wonder Moments" light installation.
- **Note**: Located in EXPOCITY, next to the Expo '70 Commemorative Park. Great for a full day out.

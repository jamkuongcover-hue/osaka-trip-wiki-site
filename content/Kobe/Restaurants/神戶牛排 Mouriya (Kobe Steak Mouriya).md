# Kobe Steak Restaurant Mouriya

A 130-year established restaurant famous for its premium aged Kobe beef.

## Details
- **Category**: Restaurants
- **City**: Kobe
- **Target Audience**: High-quality cooked food (Wagyu), Omakase-style experience.
- **Accessibility**: Sannomiya branches are generally accessible with elevators.
- **Parking**: Use nearby paid parking lots in Sannomiya.
- **Food Type**: Teppanyaki (Kobe Beef).
- **Note**: Known for strict quality standards and long history. Reservations highly recommended.
